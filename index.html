Introduction

There have been many accidents in recent years and due to the lack of communication many patients injured cannot reach the hospital on time. The main issue is on the highways where there is lack of signal.

To improve the communication & save life we have developed an app called LHE(Life Health Emergency).

Our Application

The main aim of our application is to make sure that a distress signal is sent to the near by hospital, Police and the fire department to approach the location as soon as possible. The distress signal comes from a mobile device. We are also providing online doctor appointments, Online pharmacy and delivery services

Our Initiation

When we travel on Highways or in any kind dense areas it is so hard to find signal and reaching out to our dear ones is so difficult.

Accidents cannot be predicted and foreseen. To reduce the effort of communicating the Hospital or the dear ones during the crisis we have developed this application.

Our Philosophy

Vision

We have a clear vision to provide service to all the humanity whose life is in danger. With this app we can save any human beings in any kind of Emergency.

Mission

Our Misson is to save as many lives as possible and provide the same service around the world.
